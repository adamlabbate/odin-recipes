Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a fresh, creamy dip made from ripe avocados, lime juice, and simple, flavorful ingredients. Mashed to your preferred texture and mixed with diced onion, tomato, cilantro, and a touch of salt, it delivers a bright, refreshing taste with every bite. The lime juice adds a slight tang while also helping keep the avocado vibrant and green.

Perfect as a dip for tortilla chips, a topping for tacos, or a spread for sandwiches and burgers, guacamole is quick to prepare and always a crowd-pleaser. With just a few fresh ingredients and minimal effort, itâ€™s a simple recipe that brings bold, authentic flavor to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocado in a medium serving bowl.
  3. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic.
  4. Season with lime juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
